About Our Project

This project analyzes futuristic urban traffic patterns and energy use to provide insights for smarter and more sustainable city mobility solutions.

Project Overview

This Project is about Smart Mobility and Futuristic Taffic Sustainability. The main Goal is to make city travel easier, faster, and eco-friendly by using modern technologies such as smart traffic systems, electric vehicles, ride-shaping , data-driven planning. Our project focusses on reducing traffic jams, lowering pollution, and improving transport efficiency so that people can move safely and comfortably in urban areas.

Objectives

Goal

To understand and improve urban traffic patterns and sustainability in futuristic cities. To provide actionable insights that help city planners, transportation engineers, and policymakers implement smarter, greener, and more efficient traffic management systems in urban environments of the future.

Purpose

To support sustainable urban planning by reducing congestion, optimizing energy use, and promoting eco-friendly transportation solutions.

Challenges

Challenge

Predicting rare traffic incidents (like accidents or road closures) was difficult because they occurred very rarely in the dataset.

Solution

Applied oversampling techniques and used class weighting to balance the model, which improved the detection of rare events.

Challenge

Predicting traffic density was difficult because it depends on many factors (weather, time, vehicle type, economic conditions) that interact in complex ways.

Solution

Used advanced models like Random Forest and XGBoost to capture these non-linear relationships and improve prediction accuracy.

Challenge

High Dimensionality of Data- Handling over 1.2 million records was computationally intensive

Solution

Used chunk processing, optimized data types, and feature selection to reduce memory usage and speed up training.
